Audio Player Project

In this project I wrote a class that represents a playlist of songs. My Playlist class managed a vector of song objects.

This includes adding and deleting songs. Playing from the playlist. This includes keeping track of which is the next song to be played and supporting different playing modes (such as normal and repeat).

"Playing" the song will consist of printing the song details. The driver program Player.cpp will allow you to manage multiple playlists and switch between them.
